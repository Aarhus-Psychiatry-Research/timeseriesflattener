add_spec(spec: Union[list[timeseriesflattener.feature_spec_objects._AnySpec], _AnySpec])[source]#

Add a specification to the flattened dataset.

This adds it to a queue of unprocessed specs, which are not processed until you call the .compute() or .get_df() methods. This allows us to more effectiely parallelise the processing of the specs.

Most of the complexity lies in the OutcomeSpec and PredictorSpec objects. For further documentation, see those objects and the tutorial.
